How it works

Three steps. Zero manual reading.

From broken to fixed in the time it takes to open a manual.

Step 01
📱

Point your camera

Open PhysicsCopilot and aim your phone at the machine. The live video stream starts instantly — no photo uploads, no typing.

Step 02
🧠

AI sees & diagnoses

Multimodal AI analyzes the stream in real time — identifies faults, worn parts, and misconfigurations, then retrieves the exact repair procedure.

Step 03
🔧

Follow voice guidance

Every step is read aloud. Confirm hands-free. The AI watches your progress, corrects mistakes, and moves you forward when ready.
