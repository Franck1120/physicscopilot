How it works

Three steps. Zero manual reading.

1

Point your camera

Open the app and aim your phone at the machine you need to fix. The AI stream starts instantly.

2

AI analyzes

Gemini Vision identifies the issue — clogged nozzle, belt tension, layer shifting — and retrieves the right repair procedure from the knowledge base.

3

Follow voice guidance

Each step is read aloud. Confirm each one hands-free. The AI watches your progress and moves you forward.
