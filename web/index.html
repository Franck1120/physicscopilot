How it works

Point. Stream. Fix.

Three steps between broken and working — no manual, no call center, no waiting.

📷 Point

Point your camera

Aim your phone at the machine. The live AI stream starts instantly — no setup, no friction, no QR codes.

⚡ Stream

AI streams the diagnosis

Gemini Vision identifies the fault in real time — clogged nozzle, belt tension, layer shift — and retrieves the exact repair procedure.

✅ Fix

Follow voice guidance

Each step is read aloud. Your hands stay on the machine. The AI watches your progress and moves you forward only when ready.
