Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

PhysicsCopilot ("we", "us") is an AI-powered field assistance tool. This policy explains how we handle data collected through the mobile app and web service.

2. Data we collect

  • Camera frames — sent over WebSocket to our server for real-time analysis. Frames are processed by Google Gemini and are not stored by us.
  • Session data — device type, problem description, repair steps. Stored in-memory and discarded when the session ends (no persistent DB in the current version).
  • Email address — only if you join the waitlist. Used solely to contact you about early access.
  • Server logs — request method, path, status, latency, and a hashed (SHA-256) IP address. Raw IPs are never logged.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels on the landing page.
  • We do not store biometric data or voice recordings.

4. Third-party services

  • Google Gemini API — camera frames are processed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Render — server hosting. See Render's Privacy Policy.

5. Data retention

In-memory session data is discarded automatically after 30 minutes of inactivity. Server logs are retained for 30 days.

6. Your rights

To request deletion of your waitlist email or any other data, contact us at hello@physicscopilot.app.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be announced on the website.
